About Me!

Hello! My name is Hannah Hamilton and I'm 19 years old. I'm a student at San Jacinto College and getting my associate's degree. I have made this website to show my digital artwork. Hopefully as I continue to make art, I will add more of my pieces here! This website will tell you a little bit about me, my art journey, and will show how I get my creativety out in digital form.

My Art Journey.

My art journey started when I was really little. I've been drawing ever since I was a little girl. I loved it! There was always paper in my house somewhere so, I was always drawing. Fun fact! When I was younger I absolutely loved drawing with ink pens. I rarely did drawings with pencils. It was always ink pens for me.
